About MAE

Besides giving a through grounding in basic sciences and engineering subjects the curriculum in mechanical and automation engineering emphasises on deep understanding of applied mechanics, microprocessors, machine design, heat transfer, refrigeration and air conditioning, machine tools, metrology, robotics and CAD(CAM) etc. The department has well-equipped laboratories in related fields
